Q-01: What is flooding?

Answer: Flooding is an overflow of water onto land that is normally dry. It can occur due to heavy rainfall, storm surges, melting snow, or dam failure.

Q-02: What are the types of floods?

Answer: The main types include river floods, flash floods, coastal floods, urban flooding, and overflow from lakes or reservoirs.
